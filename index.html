"Our Mission is Missions!!"

"The mission of Assemblies of God Campus Ministry is to empower students to know God, grow in their faith, and go into the world to make a difference. We are committed to reaching and transforming the lives of college students and young adults through evangelism, discipleship, leadership development, community building, and mission mobilization. Our goal is to introduce students to a personal relationship with Jesus Christ, deepen their faith, and equip them to become influential leaders in their communities and world changers for Christ. By fostering a sense of belonging and connection among students, facilitating meaningful relationships and support networks, and inspiring them to share the Gospel globally, we aim to make a lasting impact on the next generation of leaders, transforming campuses, communities, and the world for Christ."
